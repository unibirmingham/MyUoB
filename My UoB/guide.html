Advice and
Representation Centre (ARC)

The ARC provides free, impartial and confidential information, advice and representation on a wide range of issues to all students.

Whatever the question – or difficulty – we’ll always try to help and if we can’t we’re sure to know someone who can, within or outside the Guild or University.

Location: Ground Floor, Guild of Students
Email: thearc@guild.bham.ac.uk 
Tel: +44 (0)121 251 2400
Find out more: ARC website

Opening hours

Term Time: Monday–Friday, 10am–4pm
Vacation: Monday–Friday, 12noon–2pm

Counselling and Guidance

The Counselling and Guidance Service provides counselling for all registered students in order to help you engage more effectively in your academic and social environment and to be sufficiently free from psychological distress to achieve your full potential. The service also runs groups and workshops to help students adjust to life at university. 

Apart from issues associated with settling in to a new environment we also help with other problems such as depression, anxiety, flat mate issues, eating problems, and relationship difficulties.

Location: 3 Elms Road
Tel: +44 (0)121 414 5130.
Counselling and Guidance Service website

Opening hours

Term-time: Monday–Friday, 9am–5pm (Evening appointments also available during term time)

Vacation: Monday–Friday, 9am–12.30pm and 1.30pm–5pm
